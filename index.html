Our Story

It all began with a simple conversation that somehow turned into a life full of laughter, late-night talks, and adventures we never expected. Being together felt easy, joyful, and more wonderful than we could have imagined.

Then Toby and Yumi arrived, our lively and opinionated little companions. They took over the schedules, the meals, and the best spots on the couch and somehow made sure that every decision, big or small, went through them first.

Through every moment, we learned how to compromise, how to co-parent, and how to love more deeply than we ever thought possible. After so many memories, a few chewed couches, and countless shared laughs, we realized that a lifetime together with Toby and Yumi is exactly where we are meant to be.
